$\phi_{TT}$ | 3 | 3 | -1/3

$\phi_{SS}$ | $\bar{6}$ | 1 | -1/3

$\phi_{TS}$ | 3 | 1 | -1/3

$\phi_{SSu_R}$ | $\bar{6}$ | 1 | -4/3

$\phi_{TSu_R}$ | 3 | 1 | -4/3

$\phi_{SSd_R}$ | $\bar{6}$             | 1 | +2/3

$\phi_{TSd_R}$ | 3 | 1 | +2/3

$\phi_{OD}$ | 8 | 2 | +1/2
